About Me

I am a New Yorker who moved to Colorado in August 2016 for graduate school. I miss bagels, pizza, friends, and family. In Colorado, I enjoy math, hiking, dancing, and low-humidity weather.

I enjoy all levels of math, and I am available as a tutor for middle school, high school and undergraduate math students (send me an email!).

I enjoy number theory especially, ever since taking an introductory level course as an undergraduate at Sarah Lawrence College. I am so glad that I had the opportunity to see how beautiful math is beyond Calculus, and I hope to help my students see this as well.

In Fall 2018, I began my third year as a PhD student at CU Boulder. I am studying algebraic number theory with Dr. Katherine E. Stange.
